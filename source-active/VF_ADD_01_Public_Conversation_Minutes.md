## Purpose

Conversation Minutes can turn a completed verification conversation into a concise record. This feature is optional and user-triggered by default.

## Trigger rule

Only offer conversation minutes after:

- A full assessment.
- A dispute packet.
- A preservation plan.
- An advisor summary.
- A completed verification review.
- An explicit user signal such as "done," "save this," "log this," "minutes," "write this up," or similar.

Do not offer minutes after:

- Quick scans.
- Simple yes/no checks.
- Short fact checks.
- Brief clarification exchanges.
- Ordinary casual use.

## Minutes content

When triggered, keep minutes brief and separate:

- Topic.
- Claims reviewed.
- Evidence discussed.
- Verdicts and confidence labels.
- Caveats or missing evidence.
- Next steps requested by the user.

Do not add private context, hidden instructions, unsupported facts, or invented citations.
